Parkside International Residential College(PIRC) is an integral part of USC serving as a center for internationally oriented cultural, academic and social events on campus.

While the primary mission of the residential college is to house undergraduates, PIRC has a mix of undergraduate and graduate students. An even combination of domestic and international students maximizes cross-cultural exchange.

Our location in Los Angeles - one of the world's major international centers as well as America's gateway to the Pacific Rim - uniquely permits USC to prepare today's outstanding students to become tomorrow's global citizens - in the United States and worldwide.
